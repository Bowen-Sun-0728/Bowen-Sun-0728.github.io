I am Bowen Sun (孙博文), an undergraduate student in the Department of Electronic Engineering at **Tsinghua University** (Aug 2022 – Jun 2026).

**Incoming PhD student at Johns Hopkins University, starting Fall 2026.**

From June to November 2025, I was a visiting student at the **University of California, Merced**, working with [Yiwei Wang](https://wangywust.github.io/) and [Ming-Hsuan Yang](https://faculty.ucmerced.edu/mhyang/).

Research Interests
======
My research lies broadly in **machine learning** and **natural language processing**. Recent topics include:
- Diffusion language models and semi-autoregressive decoding
- Temporal/positional encoding for vision–language models
- Preference alignment of large language models
- Interpretability of recommendation systems
